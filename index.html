About Me

Hi, I’m Alicia Yu, a Denver-based software developer with a passion for building intuitive, user-focused applications that solve real-world problems. My journey into tech began with a Bachelor’s degree in Neuroscience from the University of Colorado Boulder, where I developed a deep appreciation for analytical thinking and problem-solving. Now, I channel that same curiosity and determination into crafting seamless digital experiences. I specialize in full-stack development, with experience in React, Flask, and SQLAlchemy, and enjoy exploring the intersection of technology and user experience. When I’m not coding, you can find me spending time with my dogs, Rootbeer and Oakley, and my cats, Boots and Peach, or trying out a new recipe. I’m excited about using my skills to make a meaningful impact, whether it’s through innovative fundraising technology or by empowering others with tools to succeed. Let’s connect and create something amazing!
